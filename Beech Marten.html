Beech Marten

The Marten With Many Names
The Beech Marten (Martes Foina)is no stranger to nicknames. Also known as the Stone Marten, House Marten, as well as the White Breasted Marten, the Beech Marten is widespread across Europe as well as some parts of Central Asia.
