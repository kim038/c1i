Nilgiri Marten

The Treasure Of India
The Nilgiri Marten (Martes Gwatkinsii)is very similar to the Yellow-Throated Marten in both appearance and geography. However, it is classified as its own species, and is much rarer than the Yellow-Throated Marten, being only found in South India. Also threatened by habitat loss, the Nilgiri Marten is listed as a protected species, luckily only classified as vulnerable.
